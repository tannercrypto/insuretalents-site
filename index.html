About InsureTalents

Built by someone
who knows insurance.

A boutique firm founded on deep industry knowledge and a commitment to placements that last.

We're not generalists.
We're insurance recruiters.

InsureTalents was founded with a single conviction: insurance is a specialized industry, and it deserves a specialized recruiting partner. We've spent years inside this market — not observing it from the outside — which means we understand the difference between a resume and a real producer.

Our background spans captive agent transitions, commercial lines producer recruitment, employee benefits staffing, underwriting, claims, risk management, and senior executive search. We place professionals at every level, from first-year account managers to Regional Vice Presidents.

We work with independent agencies, regional carriers, and national brokerages that are serious about building high-performing teams — and who want a recruiting partner who speaks the language of their industry.
